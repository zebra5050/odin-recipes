Mong. Beef

An asian inspired dish with American ease mixed in.

    Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Corn Starch
  • Green Onions
  • Brown Sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green Beans
  • Broccoli

      • Steps

    1. Brown Beef in medium high heat pan
    2. Mix ginger and garlic in suace pan until fragrant
    3. Add water, soy sauce, and brown sugar boil until sugar melts then simmer
    4. sautee veggies with beef then mix with sauce and let simmer for 10 minutes covered
